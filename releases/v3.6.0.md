# Tradeblocks 3.6.0

## Selection-adjusted lower bounds

`@tradeblocks/lib/calculations` now exports
`pairedSelectionAdjustedLowerBound()`, an additive inference primitive for a
selected member of a predeclared comparison family.

Every moving-block resample uses one shared index draw for the incumbent and
all eligible members. The calculation centers each member's resampled error,
takes the family maximum, and reports a one-sided simultaneous lower bound for
the selected member. The selected member may be data-dependent and does not
need to be the observed maximum.

The result carries stable caller references, member point estimates, effective
sample size, the finite-resample critical rank and resolution, and explicit
underpowered or not-comparable states. It does not expose raw bootstrap draws.

Existing paired bootstrap APIs and behavior are unchanged. Callers opt in by
calling the new function and remain responsible for binding the complete
eligible family and any domain-specific comparability rules before inference.
